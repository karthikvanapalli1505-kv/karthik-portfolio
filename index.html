Design Process

How I Work

01
Understand
Research the real situation

I start with who the user is and the exact moment they are in. Interviews and observation to understand real behaviour — not assumed behaviour.

02
Define
Structure the problem

Before touching design tools I map the user journey and find where complexity creates friction. The problem definition drives every decision.

03
Design
Build clear interfaces

Wireframes first, then UI. Each screen has one clear action. Figma prototypes to test flow decisions before committing to high-fidelity.

04
Refine
Iterate to remove friction

Test with real users — even 5 sessions surface critical issues. Each feedback round becomes a documented set of changes, not guesswork.

About Me

Who I Am

Venkata Karthik Vanapalli

I'm a UX and product designer focused on simplifying complex systems through clear, structured user experiences. My background in business and technology helps me understand both user needs and product goals — and design solutions where they align.

I enjoy designing products that guide people through important moments — sending money internationally, navigating first-time travel, or connecting communities in need.
